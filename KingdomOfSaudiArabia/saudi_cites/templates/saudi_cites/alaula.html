Activites

KHAYBAR HERITAGE TOUR

The oasis of Khaybar is one of the oldest areas of human settlement in the Arabian Peninsula. Shrouded in mystery for millennia, Khaybar is now unlocking the secrets of prehistoric Saudi Arabia. A landscape shaped by millions of years of volcanic eruptions with a lush oasis at its centre, its historical legacy has undoubtedly played a pivotal role in shaping the world around us.

KHAYBAR VOLCANO CAMP

The nature reserve of Harrat Khaybar is an ancient volcanic landscape in one of the most spectacular locations on earth. Here, some of the earliest creations of ancient Arabia continue to be rediscovered on a monumental scale - and today hosts Khaybar Volcano Camp. With this first-of-its-kind experience, guests experience the extraordinary by day and the otherworldly by night.
